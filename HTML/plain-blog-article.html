1954 The strat was born

The strat was originally designed by Leo Fender, Bill Carson, George Fullerton and Freddie Tavares. The strat had a revolutionary design for the time it was designed, it had a slim body, 3 pickups, contoured body (so it was really comfortable to use) and the best of all, it had a modular design, Leo designed the strat to make it easy to repair, you can change everything in a strat, from tuners to electronics, to pickups going as far as neck and body.
